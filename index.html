The project

We will be developing an online tool to store your passwords securely and privately.

But it’s not about the app, this is an educational project, the important part is the process of building it.

The experience of creating the tool will be documented extensively generating much more than a web application.

Can you give a hand? There are many ways of helping the project, discover how you can be part of it.
